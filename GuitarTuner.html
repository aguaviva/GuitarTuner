Ultimate Online Guitar Tuner

Quick start

  • Each bar correspond to each note in the piano scale.
  • The height of the bar determines the note's power.
  • The red lines show the notes of the 6 strings when played open.
  • The 5th string is always tricky to tune, try tunning it by placing your finger on the 10th fret, that should give you a D3.
